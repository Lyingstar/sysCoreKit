Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/17/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the developer’s Google add-ons (“Add-ons”) handle data.

Data Processing

The Add-ons only process the content of the currently open Google Document in order to provide real-time word and character counts.

Data Collection and Storage

  • No data is stored
  • No data is transmitted
  • No data is shared with any third party
  • No personal information is collected or retained

Data Usage

All processing occurs locally within the user’s Google Workspace environment, as permitted by Google’s authorization framework. Document content is accessed only temporarily and solely to perform the requested functionality.

Third-Party Access

The Add-ons do not use third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Add-ons indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions or requests, contact the developer. Contact information is on the Support & About page.
